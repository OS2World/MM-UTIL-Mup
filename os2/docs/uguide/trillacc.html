Trill with accidental

Sometimes when notating a trill, you may want to place an accidental between the "tr" and the wavy line. The typical way of getting a trill, something like 

mussym above 1: 1 "tr" til 3;

doesn't allow for such an accidental. However, the desired output can be achieved with a slightly different input: 
rom above 1: 1 "\(tr)\s(-2)\(flat)~" til 3;

The \s(-2) makes the flat a little smaller; you can adjust the actual size to your taste. The tilde at the end of the string tells Mup to use a wavy line for the til clause.
