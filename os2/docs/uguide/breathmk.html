Breath marks

A comma in a large font can be used as a breath mark. Something in the range of 20 to 30 points usually looks good; you can adjust to your preference. 

1: c;d;2e;
rom (24) above all dist 0: 4.5 ",";
bar

1: e;d;2c;
bar
